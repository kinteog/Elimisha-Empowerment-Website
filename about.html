About Us

Our Story

Elimisha Empowerment was born in 2019 out of a simple, heartfelt idea that real change begins when communities are equipped to rely on themselves. What started with a small group of university students volunteering in Nakuru has grown into a vibrant community-based organization of 15 dedicated members and a growing network of volunteers. Together we walk with young people who live on less than $3 a daynot to give handouts, but to share tools, skills, and hope. From Nakuru to Diani, we’ve built trust, nurtured local talent, and inspired thousands to discover what’s possible when opportunity meets determination.

Reducing inequalities by adopting technology for self-sustainability.

Our Vision

To develop communities by providing knowledge and skills through innovative methods of research, training, and self-awareness.

Our Mission

Our Objectives

  • To reduce dependency in the community by creating opportunities for self-reliance.
  • To promote the use of ICT through training and development.
  • To enhance local and international partnerships.
  • To integrate indigenous and contemporary knowledge for sustainable development.
