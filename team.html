Get To Know the Hearts Behind Elimisha Empowerment

Joyce is a passionate development leader with a gift for building bridges between communities and organizations. With a strong background in Organizational Development, Public Relations, and Marketing Management, she has spent over a decade nurturing relationships that bridge organizations and communities across Kenya.

Her mentorship footprint stretches across Kenya from Nairobi to Garissa to Kisumu to Diani, touching the lives of children and youth aged 5 to 24 through empowerment, guidance, and compassion. Joyce’s work reflects her lifelong commitment to reducing dependency by instilling self-belief and practical skills.

She holds a Diploma in Public Relations & Marketing Management, a Bachelor of Arts in Leadership and Management, and a Master of Development Studies (Organizational Development) from St. Paul’s University, Kenya.

“I believe every child deserves more than hope — they deserve opportunity and the skills to create their own future.”

Joyce Viata
Co-Founder & Director

Esther Kalekye Mumo is a dynamic Development Strategist and Organizational Development Specialist with over 15 years of experience driving transformative change across Africa. A visionary leader with a passion for people, systems, and purpose, Esther has worked at the intersection of governance, enterprise growth, and social impact, empowering organizations and individuals to unlock their full potential. She holds degrees in Leadership, Management and Development Studies.

Esther has championed inclusive economic programs for women and youth, blending research, mentorship, and enterprise development to create lasting impact. A published author and thought leader in participatory development and leadership transformation, Esther’s work reflects her deep belief that sustainable progress begins from the inside out with empowered people, intentional learning, and resilient systems. Whether advising boards, mentoring entrepreneurs, or shaping talent strategies, she brings insight, empathy, and excellence to every engagement.

Esther Kalekye Mumo
Co-Founder & Director

Patrick is an economist and community development practitioner. He has a vast experience of working and engaging with the community for the last 10 years. He has mediated in conflict issues with the community and wildlife in Ruma National Park, Lambwe Valley, Homabay County. Realizing the community needs and their afflictions, he has come up with sustainable programs for the youths, such as Agri-business farming on a small scale, tapping and nurturing the youth's talents, economic empowerment through household strengthening and capacity development.

This has tremendously aided in bridging the intergenerational gap for people in Suba Community, Homa Bay County, Kenya. He has also been involved in sensitizing the elderly people above 70 years who qualify for the Social Security Fund and bringing to their realization how the fund can be utilized in their well-being through Hospital Insurance Contribution (NHIF). He is also a seasoned lecturer and has taught in universities across Africa on Economic Development. Patrick holds a B.A in economics and sociology, MA in applied economics for Development from Laikipia University and PhD in Development Studies from St. Paul's University.

Patrick Asango Okanga
Director
