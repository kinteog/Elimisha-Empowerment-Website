Digital Empowerment

Technology for Change

Technology is more than a tool; it’s a bridge to opportunity. Through Technology for Change, we equip youth and women with essential digital skills that open doors to employment and entrepreneurship. From learning online marketing to coding basics, participants gain practical knowledge that helps them thrive in today’s economy.

Impact:

  • Over 1,000 youth (18–25 years) trained in digital and entrepreneurial skills
  • Increased income-generating capacity among young entrepreneurs
  • Improved confidence & community innovation

Why it matters:
Despite Kenya’s strong digital growth, 39.8% of people still live below the poverty line, and the digital divide continues to limit rural opportunities. Our program bridges that gap by making technology an equalizer and not a privilege.

Community Welfare

Nourish & Flourish

No one can thrive on an empty stomach. Nourish & Flourish ensures families in our communities have access to food, clothing, and dignity. Every year, we organize food and clothing drives that reach the most vulnerable members especially during tough economic times or drought.

Impact:

  • Annual food and clothes drives reaching hundreds of households
  • Better nutrition and improved wellbeing for families in need
  • Strengthened community bonds through collective giving

Why it matters:
According to Kenya’s 2024 poverty report, 31.7% of Kenyans face food poverty meaning they cannot meet daily nutritional needs. Nourish & Flourish responds to that reality with compassion and action.

Economic Empowerment

Household Strengthening

Strong households build strong communities. This program helps families acquire skills and knowledge to become self-reliant. Through capacity-building workshops on financial literacy, savings, and small business management, we equip households to plan, save, and grow.

Impact:

  • 200+ households supported through financial literacy and business mentoring
  • Families shifting from aid to income generation
  • Growing sense of dignity and ownership in the community

Why it matters:
Sustainable change happens when people have both the skills and the mindset to shape their future. Household Strengthening empowers families to do just that.

Health & Dignity

Pads for Life

Dignity should never be a privilege. Pads for Life provides girls and women with sanitary towels and menstrual health education, ensuring they can attend school and run businesses without shame or interruption.

Impact:

  • 500+ girls and women supported annually
  • Improved school attendance & self-esteem among girls
  • Open conversations breaking menstrual stigma in communities

Why it matters:
Access to menstrual hygiene is not just a health issue, it’s a human rights issue. Through Pads for Life, we keep girls in school and women in business.
